Origin Story

Originally started in the alleys of New York City, on a stormy cold rainy night and Mrs. Wong was closing up her stand. When a stanger asked if he could get a bowl of hot soup. She started the fire again, she made "fresh" hot soup, fixed the stranger a togo bowl, gave him the soup and the man went home. Three days later the man came back and said this was the best soup I ever tasted in my life! This is how the Lengend of Mrs. Wong"s FAMOUS FOOD began!
